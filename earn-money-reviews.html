How Our Review Rewards Work

AnalyzeG offers a transparent reward system for reviewers who provide authentic, helpful video content. Our blockchain-based verification ensures trust between brands and reviewers, while our payment system guarantees fair compensation for your time and insights. You receive 50% of the selling price when your review is sold to brands.

Simple 3-Step Process

  1. Sign Up: Create your AnalyzeG account and complete your reviewer profile with your interests and expertise.
  2. Record Reviews: Choose available products or services that match your profile and create honest video reviews.
  3. Get Paid: Wait for your review to be sold. Once sold, receive 50% of the selling price directly to your preferred account in Indian Rupees.

Why Reviewers Choose AnalyzeG

  • Competitive Compensation: Earn between ₹100 to ₹500 per review based on the selling price.

  • Blockchain Verification: Your reviews are permanently recorded and tamper-proof.

  • Professional Growth: Build your reputation as a trusted reviewer in your field.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I earn per review?

Payment varies depending on product complexity, review length, and expertise required. Reviewers typically earn between ₹100 to ₹500 per approved and sold video review, which is 50% of the selling price.

Basic equipment is fine to start! A smartphone with a decent camera and microphone is sufficient for most reviews.

You'll receive payment after your review is sold to a brand. The waiting time varies depending on market demand for your specific review content.
