Frequently Asked Questions About AnalyzeG

Get answers to common questions about the AnalyzeG platform

What is AnalyzeG?

AnalyzeG is a revolutionary blockchain-based platform that connects reviewers with brands. Our platform allows users to create authentic video reviews while earning rewards through a transparent and secure system. AnalyzeG uses blockchain technology to verify the authenticity of reviews, building trust between consumers and brands.

How does AnalyzeG work?

AnalyzeG operates through a simple three-step process:

  1. Review Creation: Users select products or services from our catalog and create honest video reviews.
  2. Verification Process: Our blockchain system verifies the authenticity and uniqueness of each review.
  3. Monetization: Once reviews are sold to brands, reviewers earn 50% of the selling price in Indian Rupees.

When will AnalyzeG launch?

AnalyzeG is currently in development and will be launching soon. We're working hard to create the most secure, user-friendly platform possible. Sign up on our homepage to be notified when we launch and to get early access to the platform.

Why is AnalyzeG different from other review platforms?

AnalyzeG stands out from other review platforms for several key reasons:

  • Blockchain Verification: Our blockchain technology ensures all reviews are authentic and tamper-proof.
  • Video-Based: We focus exclusively on video reviews, which provide more authentic and detailed feedback.
  • Fair Compensation: Reviewers receive 50% of the revenue when their review is sold to a brand.
  • Data Privacy: We maintain strict privacy standards and transparent data practices.

How much can I earn with AnalyzeG?

Reviewers on AnalyzeG can earn between ₹100 to ₹500 per review depending on factors like review quality, product category, and market demand. As your reputation grows on the platform, you may qualify for premium review opportunities with higher compensation rates.

Who can use AnalyzeG?

AnalyzeG is designed for two main user groups:

  • Reviewers: Anyone with a smartphone can create video reviews and earn money.
  • Brands: Companies looking for authentic consumer feedback can purchase reviews through our platform.

We welcome users from all demographics and geographic locations, making our platform accessible to everyone.
